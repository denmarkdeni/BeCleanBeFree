about The App

Be Clean Be Free,

BeCleanBeFree is an innovative anti-drug awareness and recovery application designed to support the Excise Department in combating drug abuse through technology-driven engagement. This mobile/web-based platform aims to educate the public about the dangers of drug use, facilitate anonymous reporting of drug-related activities, and provide vital recovery and rehabilitation resources. The app offers an Awareness Section featuring educational material, real-life stories, and multimedia content, alongside a Reporting Module that enables secure and confidential submissions of drug use or trafficking incidents.

To aid individuals seeking help, the Help and Resources module provides a directory of rehabilitation centers and hotline contacts. The Self-Assessment Quiz encourages self-reflection and learning, while the News Section keeps users updated on anti-drug campaigns and enforcement actions.
